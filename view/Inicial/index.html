ByteFlow - MIPS Simulator


ByteFlow is a simulation tool focused on studying MIPS architecture. It allows step-by-step execution of Assembly programs and offers features such as:

  • Reading MIPS instructions and setting execution parameters.
  • Graphical visualization of memory and register states at each clock cycle.
  • Monitoring of the PC register and representation of the current instruction in binary or hexadecimal.
  • Calculation of the total execution time of the program.

ByteFlow is designed to help understand the internal workings of MIPS processors, making it a useful tool for students and professionals in the field of computer architecture.
